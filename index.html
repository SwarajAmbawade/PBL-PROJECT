About the website

Every year about 5.2 million people lose their lives due to human errors and half or zero knowledge about the medical history of the patients in critical situations. In emergency conditions, carrying the files, doing medical tests cost a lot of time, loss of money, and sometimes can compromise the health of the patient. Medikeeps is the best solution to these problems. You can store all your medical history in just one click, and can save your and other’s lives in emergency situations.
